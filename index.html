MADE Export Portal

My Australian Defence Exports

A streamlined pathway from registration to authorised exports.

The MADE portal mirrors Defence’s workflows so exporters can progress confidently—submit applications, collaborate with DEC, and keep stakeholders informed.

  • Guided onboarding aligned with Defence guidance
  • Smart validation for goods, end-use and licensing
  • Live status sharing with partners and government

How to use the MADE portal

  1. Getting started: confirm business profile, digital identity and security contacts.
  2. Navigate the portal: personalise dashboards, invite collaborators and link Navigator support.
  3. Prepare your export: build applications with templated goods lists and contextual guidance.
  4. DEC liaison & tracking: share reference IDs, respond to queries and upload supporting artefacts.
  5. Authorised AUS/UK/US community: surface licence conditions, share compliance packs and mark fulfilment.
